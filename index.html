What is This Website?

This website is dedicated to raising public awareness of environmental protection and climate change. We believe that through education and action, everyone can contribute to protecting our planet.

Global warming is one of the toughest challenges facing the world today. From the melting of polar glaciers to the frequent occurrence of extreme weather events, the impact of climate change has become ubiquitous. We need to take immediate action to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and protect the ecosystem. Climate change Video.

Our Mission

As students majoring in environmental management, we created this website to transform what we have learned in class into practical actions. We believe that knowledge should serve society and environmental protection requires the participation of everyone.
